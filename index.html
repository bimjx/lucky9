1. Press the Start button to begin the game.

2. Click the Reveal button to show the cards and the total.

3. The closer your total is to 9, the higher your chances of winning.

4. If you're satisfied with the total value, click Stand to pass the turn to the next player.

5. If not, click the Hit button to draw another card. You can have a maximum of three cards only.

6. Click Continue to proceed or Start to restart the game.
