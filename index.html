How to play!
1. Click the start button to draw 2 random cards.
2. The "total" displays the sum of your cards. The closer it is to 9, the more chances you win.
3. If you are satisfied, click the stand button to pass the turn to the other player.
4. If you are not satisfied, click hit to draw another card. Take note that only three cards are allowed.
